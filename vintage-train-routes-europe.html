â“ Frequently Asked Questions

Are these real vintage trains or replicas?

The Venice Simplon-Orient-Express uses authentic 1920s-30s carriages fully restored. Others like Royal Scotsman use vintage rolling stock (1960s-80s) redesigned with period interiors. Swiss scenic trains are modern with panoramic features.

Do I need a rail pass?

Luxury heritage trains are standalone experiences â€” no rail pass accepted. For Swiss scenic routes (Glacier/Bernina Express), a Swiss Travel Pass covers the route but you must pay seat reservations separately.

Can I bring luggage?

Overnight luxury trains have cabin luggage allowances (typically 2 bags). Day scenic trains have overhead racks. Large suitcases may require luggage forwarding services.

Are children welcome?

Orient Express allows children 4+ but it's adult-oriented. Royal Scotsman requires guests 12+. Scenic routes like Glacier Express are family-friendly with no age restrictions.
