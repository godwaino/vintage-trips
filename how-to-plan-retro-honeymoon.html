Step 2: Balance Vintage Aesthetics with Modern Comfort

Retro doesn't mean sacrificing comfort. Look for:

  • Historic properties with renovated amenities: 1920s hotel with original Art Deco details + modern bathrooms and Wi-Fi
  • Heritage trains with contemporary safety: Orient Express has 1920s interiors but 21st-century engineering
  • Classic destinations, modern experiences: Paris-Venice-Vienna rail journey with boutique hotel stays, not backpacker hostels

Red flags: Properties that are "authentic" but uncomfortable (no heating, shared baths). Your honeymoon should be romantic, not rustic.

Step 9: Budget Realistically

Retro luxury honeymoons range $15,000-40,000 for 10-14 days, depending on:

  • Train type: Orient Express = $5,000-10,000 for 1-2 nights alone

Where to splurge: Signature train journey, one palace hotel, milestone dinner
